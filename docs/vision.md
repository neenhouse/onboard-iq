# OnboardIQ -- Vision

## North Star

Reduce engineering time-to-productivity from months to weeks by replacing ad-hoc onboarding with a structured, measurable, and personalized platform.

## Problem

Engineering onboarding at most companies is:

1. **Unstructured** -- new hires get a Confluence page and a "good luck."
2. **Role-agnostic** -- a backend engineer and a frontend engineer receive the same checklist.
3. **Unmeasured** -- managers have no visibility into onboarding progress or bottlenecks.
4. **Disconnected** -- access provisioning, mentor assignment, and milestone tracking live in separate tools (Jira, Slack, Okta, spreadsheets).
5. **One-size-fits-all** -- no adaptation based on seniority, team, or tech stack.

## Solution

OnboardIQ is a single platform that unifies every dimension of engineering onboarding:

- **Role-based checklists** that adapt to team, seniority, and tech stack.
- **Codebase walkthrough generation** that produces guided tours of relevant repositories.
- **Access provisioning tracking** so new hires and managers see what is pending, granted, or blocked.
- **Mentor matching** based on skills, availability, and team proximity.
- **30/60/90 day milestones** with clear success criteria and progress indicators.
- **Knowledge check quizzes** to validate understanding at each phase.
- **Time-to-productivity dashboards** that give managers aggregate and per-hire metrics.

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over cleverness** -- every screen should be immediately understandable.
2. **Progressive disclosure** -- show what matters now, hide what matters later.
3. **Measurable outcomes** -- every feature must produce data that feeds the metrics dashboard.
4. **Role-first personalization** -- the platform adapts to the engineer, not the other way around.
5. **Zero-config start** -- a new hire logs in and sees their personalized plan immediately.
6. **Manager visibility** -- managers get real-time insight without micromanaging.

## Target Users

| Persona | Needs |
|---------|-------|
| **New Hire Engineer** | Clear path, no ambiguity, quick access, validation of progress |
| **Engineering Manager** | Visibility into onboarding health, bottleneck identification |
| **Mentor** | Structured mentorship framework, clear expectations |
| **IT/Admin** | Access provisioning status, audit trail |
| **HR/People Ops** | Onboarding completion rates, time-to-productivity trends |

## Success Metrics

- **Time to first commit** reduced by 40%
- **Time to first production deploy** reduced by 30%
- **Onboarding satisfaction score** (NPS) above 60
- **Checklist completion rate** above 90% at 30-day mark
- **Access provisioning time** under 24 hours for all standard tools
